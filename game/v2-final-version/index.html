The Dice Game

The Dice Game Rules

1. Choose a player number between two people.


2. When you press start, a random player will get to roll the dice first.


3. Roll the dice and decide if you want to risk to roll again or pass the turn to be safe.


4. If a player rolls a one from one of the dice, the player loses rights to roll instant and the other player gets to roll.


5. If a player rolls two ones, that player's score gets set to 0 and the other player receives the chance to roll.


6. When the total score reach 30 or above, you win the game!
